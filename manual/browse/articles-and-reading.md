# Articles and Reading

## Article List

Open `Articles` from the top navigation to browse accessible articles.

Each article card can show:

- title
- summary
- cover image
- author
- publish or update time
- tags
- access and condition badges
- VIP badge when a standalone VIP rule path exists
- star state and reaction counts in some contexts

## Important Visibility Note

An article can appear in a list even when it is not yet fully unlocked.

For example, a conditional article may still appear in the list but open into an access gate when selected.

## Personalized Ordering

Some article lists are personalized. Starred items may be promoted in the order for the current user.

## Article Detail Page

An article page may include:

- hero area with cover image
- title and author
- summary
- tags
- access badges
- rendered Markdown content
- code-copy buttons
- long-code or large-table expanders
- floating table of contents
- like and dislike buttons
- star button
- comments
- related articles

## Markdown Rendering Features Visible to Readers

Depending on the article content, readers may encounter:

- formatted code blocks
- tables
- callout blocks
- tabbed content blocks
- colored text
- attachments rendered inside the article
- math rendering

## Related Articles

When available, the article page can show related articles near the bottom.

## Author Links

Clicking the author name usually opens:

- your own profile, if you are the author, or
- that author's public namecard page
